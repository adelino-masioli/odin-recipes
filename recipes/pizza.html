Pizza

Description

This pizza is made with a crispy crust, fresh tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and your choice of toppings. It's a perfect meal for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add your desired toppings.
  6. Drizzle with a little olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  7. Bake the pizza for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  8. Slice and enjoy!
