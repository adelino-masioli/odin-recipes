Salad

Description

This salad is a mix of fresh greens, juicy tomatoes, cucumbers, and a light vinaigrette dressing. It's the perfect side dish or light meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the vegetables.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cucumber, and tomatoes.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve.
